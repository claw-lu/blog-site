This represents the largest coordinated strike operation since the conflict began. Lebanon’s government declared a national day of mourning and condemned Israel’s “devastating” attacks. 世界银行发出警告，称该地区的持续冲突可能造成广泛的经济损失。银行经济学家表示，中东地区的不稳定已经对全球供应链和能源市场产生了重大影响。霍尔木兹海峡的关闭威胁着全球石油贸易，可能导致能源价格大幅上涨。世界银行预测，如果冲突继续升级，该地区的GDP可能出现显著下降。银行呼吁国际社会采取措施缓解紧张局势，以防止更大的经济灾难。世界银行还强调了通过外交手段解决冲突的重要性，以维护全球经济稳定。

The World Bank issued a warning that ongoing conflicts in the region could cause widespread economic losses. Bank economists stated that instability in the Middle East has already significantly impacted global supply chains and energy markets. The closure of the Strait of Hormuz threatens global oil trade and could lead to substantial increases in energy prices. The World Bank predicts that if conflicts continue to escalate, the region’s GDP could see significant decline. The bank called on the international community to take measures to ease tensions and prevent greater economic disaster. The World Bank also emphasized the importance of resolving conflicts through diplomatic means to maintain global economic stability.

石油价格因美伊停火而下跌，股市上涨

半岛电视台

美伊停火协议的宣布导致国际油价下跌，而华尔街和欧洲股市则出现反弹。投资者对地区紧张局势的缓解表示欢迎，认为这将有助于稳定全球能源供应。布伦特原油价格下跌超过3%，而美国西德克萨斯中质原油也出现类似跌幅。股市方面，美国主要股指均出现上涨，欧洲股市也跟随上涨趋势。然而，分析师警告称，停火协议的脆弱性意味着市场仍存在波动风险。如果停火协议破裂，油价可能迅速反弹，股市也可能出现剧烈调整。

The announcement of the US-Iran ceasefire agreement led to a decline in international oil prices, while Wall Street and European stock markets rebounded. Investors welcomed the easing of regional tensions, believing it will help stabilize global energy supplies. Brent crude oil prices fell more than 3%, while US West Texas Intermediate crude saw similar declines. In stock markets, major US indices all rose, with European markets following the upward trend. However, analysts warned that the fragility of the ceasefire agreement means markets still face volatility risks. If the ceasefire agreement breaks down, oil prices could quickly rebound and stock markets could experience sharp adjustments.

Asharq Al-Awsat

由于对脆弱停火协议的担忧，海湾地区大部分股市出现下跌。投资者对停火协议的持久性表示怀疑，特别是在以色列继续对黎巴嫩进行军事行动的情况下。科威特股市的跌幅尤为明显，投资者密切关注地区发展动态。分析师表示，海湾股市的表现反映了市场对地区稳定性的担忧。石油公司股票受到特别关注，因为霍尔木兹海峡的状况直接影响石油运输。尽管停火协议带来了短期缓解，但市场仍对长期稳定性持谨慎态度。

Most Gulf region stock markets declined due to concerns over the fragile ceasefire agreement. Investors expressed doubts about the durability of the ceasefire agreement, particularly as Israel continues military operations against Lebanon. Kuwait’s stock market showed particularly notable declines, with investors closely monitoring regional developments. Analysts stated that Gulf stock market performance reflects market concerns about regional stability. Oil company stocks received particular attention, as the situation in the Strait of Hormuz directly affects oil transportation. Despite the ceasefire agreement providing short-term relief, markets remain cautious about long-term stability.

埃克森美孚因中东紧张局势损失6%的石油产量

Asharq Al-Awsat

美国石油巨头埃克森美孚公司表示，由于中东紧张局势的影响，该公司损失了6%的石油产量。公司官员表示，霍尔木兹海峡的不确定性和地区冲突迫使其暂停部分海上作业。这一损失相当于每天约24万桶原油的产量。埃克森美孚强调，公司正在密切监控地区局势发展，并采取必要措施保护员工安全和资产安全。其他国际石油公司也面临类似挑战，整个行业都在评估地区冲突对其运营的长期影响。

US oil giant ExxonMobil stated that it has lost 6% of its oil production due to Middle East tensions. Company officials said that uncertainty in the Strait of Hormuz and regional conflicts forced it to suspend some offshore operations. This loss is equivalent to approximately 240,000 barrels of crude oil production per day. ExxonMobil emphasized that the company is closely monitoring regional developments and taking necessary measures to protect employee safety and asset security. Other international oil companies face similar challenges, with the entire industry assessing the long-term impact of regional conflicts on their operations.
