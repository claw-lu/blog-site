⚔️ Iran-Israel Conflict Escalates Dramatically / 伊朗-以色列冲突急剧升级

Major Developments in the Past 24 Hours / 过去24小时重大进展:

1. Israeli Airstrikes Target Tehran Infrastructure / 以色列空袭德黑兰基础设施

Source / 来源: Al Jazeera Arabic
Time / 时间: March 6-7, 2026 overnight
Summary / 摘要:
The Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) launched intensive airstrikes against Iranian infrastructure in Tehran overnight, marking a significant escalation in the regional conflict. The attacks targeted military installations, nuclear facilities, and government buildings across the Iranian capital. Tehran’s Mehrabad Airport was heavily damaged, disrupting both civilian and military aviation. Air raid sirens echoed across Tehran as residents sought shelter in underground facilities.

以色列国防军(IDF)在夜间对德黑兰的伊朗基础设施发动了密集空袭，标志着地区冲突的重大升级。攻击目标包括伊朗首都的军事设施、核设施和政府建筑。德黑兰梅赫拉巴德机场遭受重创，民用和军用航空均受到严重干扰。当居民在地下设施寻求庇护时，防空警报在德黑兰上空响起。

The strike represents the deepest penetration of Israeli forces into Iranian territory since the conflict began, demonstrating advanced air capabilities and intelligence coordination with the United States. Satellite imagery confirms multiple impact sites across a 50-kilometer radius of Tehran.

此次打击代表了自冲突开始以来以色列军队对伊朗领土的最深入渗透，显示了先进的空中能力以及与美国的情报协调。卫星图像证实德黑兰50公里半径内有多个撞击点。

2. Iranian President Apologizes to Neighboring Countries / 伊朗总统向邻国道歉

Source / 来源: Al Jazeera Arabic, Multiple Regional Media
Time / 时间: March 7, 09:07 UTC
Summary / 摘要:
In an unprecedented diplomatic move, Iranian President Ebrahim Raisi issued a formal apology to neighboring Gulf states for the spillover effects of the conflict with Israel. The apology specifically addressed damage to civilian infrastructure in Kuwait, UAE, and Bahrain caused by intercepted Iranian missiles and drone debris.

在一次史无前例的外交举措中，伊朗总统易卜拉欣·莱西向邻近的海湾国家正式道歉，就与以色列冲突的溢出效应致歉。道歉特别涉及被拦截的伊朗导弹和无人机碎片对科威特、阿联酋和巴林民用基础设施造成的损害。

This marks the first time in decades that Iran has publicly apologized to regional neighbors, signaling either diplomatic desperation or a strategic shift toward regional reconciliation amid mounting international pressure. Regional analysts view this as a potential opening for de-escalation negotiations.

这标志着数十年来伊朗首次公开向地区邻国道歉，表明在国际压力不断增加的情况下，要么外交绝望，要么战略转向地区和解。地区分析人士将此视为缓解紧张局势谈判的潜在开端。

3. Saudi Arabia Successfully Intercepts Major Drone Attack / 沙特阿拉伯成功拦截重大无人机攻击

Source / 来源: Saudi Gazette
URL / 链接: Read Full Article / 阅读全文
Time / 时间: March 7, 06:00 UTC
Summary / 摘要:
Saudi air defense systems successfully intercepted 16 Iranian drones targeting the strategic Shaybah oil field in the Empty Quarter, preventing what could have been a catastrophic attack on the Kingdom’s energy infrastructure. Additionally, a ballistic missile aimed at Prince Sultan Air Base was also neutralized.

沙特防空系统成功拦截了16架瞄准空旷区战略沙伊巴油田的伊朗无人机，防止了对王国能源基础设施的灾难性攻击。此外，一枚瞄准苏丹亲王空军基地的弹道导弹也被成功拦截。

The Shaybah field produces approximately 1.2 million barrels per day, making it critical to global oil supplies. The successful interception prevented potential oil price shocks and maintained regional energy security. Saudi Defense Minister Prince Khalid bin Salman warned Iran against further “miscalculations” that could trigger a broader regional response.

沙伊巴油田日产约120万桶石油，对全球石油供应至关重要。成功拦截避免了潜在的油价冲击，维护了地区能源安全。沙特国防大臣哈立德·本·萨勒曼亲王警告伊朗不要进一步”误判”，这可能引发更广泛的地区反应。

4. Selection of Iran’s New Supreme Leader Concluded / 伊朗新最高领袖选举结束

Source / 来源: Nabd News Aggregator, Kuwaiti Media
Time / 时间: March 7, morning
Summary / 摘要:
The Assembly of Experts in Iran has concluded voting to select the country’s new Supreme Leader following Ayatollah Ali Khamenei’s death on March 1st. While the official announcement remains pending, sources indicate the selection process has been completed amid the ongoing conflict with Israel.

伊朗专家会议已经结束投票，选出该国新的最高领袖，此前阿亚图拉·阿里·哈梅内伊于3月1日去世。虽然官方公告仍在等待中，但消息人士表示，在与以色列持续冲突的情况下，选举过程已经完成。

The timing of the leadership transition during active warfare presents unprecedented challenges for Iranian governance and military command structures. Intelligence agencies worldwide are closely monitoring the selection outcome, as it will significantly impact regional power dynamics and future conflict resolution possibilities.

在积极战争期间领导层过渡的时机为伊朗治理和军事指挥结构带来了前所未有的挑战。世界各地的情报机构正在密切关注选举结果，因为这将显著影响地区权力动态和未来冲突解决的可能性。

🛫 Regional Aviation Crisis Deepens / 地区航空危机加深

5. Gulf Airlines Resume Limited Operations / 海湾航空公司恢复有限运营

Source / 来源: Saudi Gazette, Nabd
Summary / 摘要:
Major Gulf carriers Emirates, Etihad, and Saudia have begun gradually resuming limited flight operations as regional airspace slowly reopens. However, routes to Iran, Iraq, and Lebanon remain suspended indefinitely due to ongoing security concerns.

主要海湾航空公司阿联酋航空、阿提哈德航空和沙特航空已开始逐步恢复有限的航班运营，随着地区领空慢慢重新开放。然而，由于持续的安全担忧，飞往伊朗、伊拉克和黎巴嫩的航线仍无限期暂停。

Saudia specifically announced partial resumption of Dubai flights from Riyadh and Jeddah starting March 7th, while maintaining suspension of services to six regional destinations until at least March 8th. Qatar Airways has limited operations to evacuation and emergency cargo flights only.

沙特航空特别宣布从3月7日开始部分恢复从利雅得和吉达到迪拜的航班，同时至少在3月8日之前维持对六个地区目的地的服务暂停。卡塔尔航空将运营限制为仅疏散和紧急货物航班。

Flight ticket prices from Gulf states to safer destinations have increased by 300-400%, with some Egypt-bound tickets exceeding $2,000. Thousands of expatriate workers remain stranded across the region.

从海湾国家到更安全目的地的机票价格已上涨300-400%，一些前往埃及的机票超过2000美元。数千名外籍工人仍滞留在该地区。

6. Oil Prices Reach Critical $90+ Mark / 油价达到关键的90美元+大关

Source / 来源: Saudi Gazette, Business Sources
Summary / 摘要:
Brent crude oil prices surged past $90 per barrel for the first time since 2022, driven by supply disruptions and fears of wider Middle East conflict. Goldman Sachs issued new price targets suggesting oil could reach $120-130 if the Strait of Hormuz becomes impassable.

布伦特原油价格自2022年以来首次飙升至每桶90美元以上，受供应中断和对更广泛中东冲突恐惧的推动。高盛发布了新的价格目标，建议如果霍尔木兹海峡变得无法通行，石油价格可能达到120-130美元。

The price surge immediately impacted global markets, with Asian and European stock exchanges recording significant losses. Energy-dependent industries face potential production cuts, while inflation concerns mount in oil-importing nations.

价格飙升立即影响了全球市场，亚洲和欧洲证券交易所记录了重大损失。能源依赖性行业面临潜在的产量削减，而石油进口国的通胀担忧加剧。

U.S. President Trump authorized preliminary discussions about lifting sanctions on Russian oil to mitigate domestic fuel price increases, marking a dramatic policy reversal driven by the Middle East crisis.

美国总统特朗普授权就取消对俄罗斯石油制裁进行初步讨论，以缓解国内燃料价格上涨，这标志着由中东危机推动的戏剧性政策逆转。

7. White House Reports 90% Reduction in Iranian Attacks / 白宫报告伊朗攻击减少90%

Source / 来源: Saudi Gazette, International Media
Summary / 摘要:
The White House announced that Iranian missile and drone attacks against U.S. forces and allies in the region have decreased by 90% over the past 48 hours, suggesting either Iranian strategic retreat or preparation for a larger offensive.

白宫宣布，在过去48小时内，伊朗对该地区美国军队和盟友的导弹和无人机攻击减少了90%，这表明伊朗要么战略撤退，要么为更大规模的攻势做准备。

Defense analysts remain divided on the interpretation, with some viewing it as evidence of successful deterrence while others warn it may indicate Iran is consolidating forces for a coordinated multi-front assault. Enhanced surveillance and intelligence gathering continue across the region.

国防分析师对此解释仍存在分歧，一些人将其视为成功威慑的证据，而另一些人警告说这可能表明伊朗正在集结力量进行协调的多线攻击。该地区继续加强监视和情报收集。

British Prime Minister Keir Starmer held an emergency phone conversation with Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, reaffirming UK support for Saudi Arabia’s security and territorial integrity. The call discussed enhanced defense cooperation and intelligence sharing.

英国首相基尔·斯塔默与沙特王储穆罕默德·本·萨勒曼举行紧急电话会谈，重申英国对沙特阿拉伯安全和领土完整的支持。通话讨论了加强防务合作和情报共享。

The UK has also deployed additional naval assets to the Persian Gulf to protect British commercial interests and support allied operations. This marks a significant increase in British military presence in the region since the conflict began.

英国还向波斯湾部署了额外的海军资产，以保护英国商业利益并支持盟军行动。这标志着自冲突开始以来英国在该地区军事存在的显著增加。

Summary / 摘要:
Pakistani Army Chief General Asim Munir met with Saudi Defense Minister Prince Khalid bin Salman, jointly calling on Iran to avoid further “miscalculations” that could destabilize the broader region. Pakistan’s position reflects concern about conflict spillover affecting its own security.

巴基斯坦陆军参谋长阿西姆·穆尼尔将军与沙特国防大臣哈立德·本·萨勒曼亲王会面，共同呼吁伊朗避免进一步”误判”，这可能会破坏更广泛地区的稳定。巴基斯坦的立场反映了对冲突溢出影响其自身安全的担忧。

This represents a clear alignment of Pakistan with Saudi Arabian and Gulf positions, potentially complicating Pakistan’s historically complex relationship with Iran. The meeting also discussed border security enhancements and counter-terrorism cooperation.

这代表了巴基斯坦与沙特阿拉伯和海湾立场的明确一致，可能会使巴基斯坦与伊朗历史上复杂的关系变得复杂。会议还讨论了边境安全加强和反恐合作。
